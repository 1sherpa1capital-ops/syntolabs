## 3. Sales Process: CLOSE Framework

### Overview: The CLOSE Method

We use the CLOSE framework for all sales conversations:

| Stage | Purpose | Key Actions |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **C - Connect** | Build rapport and trust | Personalized research, value-first approach |
| **L - Listen** | Understand their world | Discovery questions, pain identification |
| **O - Objective** | Define shared goals | Align on outcomes, set expectations |
| **S - Simulate** | Show, don't tell | Case studies, demos, prototype preview |
| **E - Execute** | Clear next steps | Proposal, signature, kickoff |

---

### Stage 1: Discovery Call (30 minutes)
**Goal:** Qualify fit, understand pain, build trust

#### CLOSE Framework in Action

**C - Connect (2 min)**
```
"Thanks for taking the time, [Name]. I know you're busy, so I want to respect your time.

I've spent some time researching [Company] and noticed [specific observation from research].
I have a few ideas, but I want to hear from you first.

Sound good?"
```

**L - Listen (12 min)**
**Discovery Questions:**
- "What's the most repetitive task your team does?"
- "If you could automate one thing, what would it be?"
- "What's this costing you in time or money?"
- "What happens if nothing changes in 6 months?"
- "On a scale of 1-10, how important is solving this right now?"

**O - Objective (3 min)**
```
"Based on what you've shared, it sounds like [summary of pain].

If we could solve this in 48 hours with a custom workflow that [specific outcome],
would that be worth [price range] to you?"
```

**S - Simulate (8 min)**
```
"Let me show you something we built for [similar company] with the exact same challenge..."

[Share case study or quick demo]

"See, we don't do cookie-cutter stuff. Everything we build is custom to your workflow,
integrated with your existing tools, and delivered in 48 hours."
```

**E - Execute (5 min)**
```
"Great, I think we can definitely help.

Here's what I propose:
1. I'll send you a custom proposal within 24 hours
2. We can review it together
3. If it makes sense, we can start as early as this week

Sound good?"
```

#### Qualifying Framework: BANT-U

| Criteria | Question | Pass/Fail |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Budget** | "What kind of budget are you working with?" | $5k+ |
| **Authority** | "Who else needs to sign off on this?" | Decision maker in loop |
| **Need** | "What's the cost of NOT solving this?" | Clear pain quantified |
| **Timeline** | "When do you need this live?" | Within 30 days |
| **Urgency** | "Why now? Why not 6 months from now?" | Compelling reason |

#### Red Flags (Deal Breakers)
- No urgency ("we'll figure it out later")
- Budget <$3k
- Looking for off-the-shelf solutions (we're custom)
- Decision maker not on the call
- Wants RFP/vendor process (we're not a commodity vendor)

---

### Stage 2: Proposal (24 hours)
**Goal:** Custom scope, pricing, timeline

**Proposal Structure:**
1. **Problem:** What we heard (parrot back their words)
2. **Impact:** What happens if nothing changes (quantify cost)
3. **Solution:** What we'll build (specific to their workflow)
4. **Timeline:** When they'll see results (48 hours to prototype)
5. **Pricing:** Custom quote (not tiered)
6. **Risk Reversal:** 30-day support included

**Pricing Philosophy:**
- Value-based: Price based on impact, not hours
- Custom per project: No cookie-cutter packages
- Range: $5k-$15k typical, $2.5k for discovery-only

---
