# Syntolabs Landing Page Overhaul Plan

## Goal
Transform the current voice-AI-focused landing page into a general AI solutions company positioning, emphasizing workflow automation, custom AI implementation, and multi-agent systems.

## Current State Analysis
- **Hero**: Voice AI receptionist messaging
- **Problem**: Missed calls/voicemail focus
- **Services**: 5 voice AI features
- **Comparison**: Old way vs AI receptionist
- **Ad Spend Protection**: Meta/Instagram ad focus
- **ROI Calculator**: Lead recovery calculations
- **Industries**: Service businesses/med spas
- **Footer**: Contact form with voice AI context

## Target Positioning
**"Your AI Implementation Partner for Workflow Automation"**
- Custom AI solutions (not cookie-cutter)
- Multi-agent systems
- 48-hour implementation
- Integration with existing tools
- 4+ shipped products as proof
